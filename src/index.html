About HMC

Welcome to our exquisite world of luxury yachting, where opulence meets the open sea. At HMC, we are proud purveyors of both motor and sailing yachts, offering a distinguished collection that epitomizes elegance, innovation, and unparalleled craftsmanship. With a rich history rooted in a passion for maritime excellence, our journey began 17 years ago when a group of avid yachting enthusiasts set out to redefine the industry.

From those early days, we have evolved into a premier destination for those seeking the epitome of nautical indulgence. Our commitment to delivering unparalleled quality, cutting-edge design, and a seamless fusion of technology and tradition has propelled us to the forefront of the yacht market. Whether you're drawn to the power and precision of a motor yacht or the timeless allure of a sailing vessel, HMC invites you to embark on a journey of unparalleled luxury and adventure. Welcome aboard!
